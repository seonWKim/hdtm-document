SAA

Description​

The SAA (Sergeant At Arms) is responsible for managing meeting logistics, ensuring the venue is prepared, and handling the club's supplies. The SAA works closely with the Treasurer to maintain necessary materials for meetings.

Roles​

  • Book the venue:
    • Ensure the meeting venue is reserved and set up.
  • Manage basic HDTM supplies with the Treasurer:
    • Maintain and organize supplies such as ribbons, ballot sheets, timing devices, banners, etc.
